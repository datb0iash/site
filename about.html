▸ Yo, I'm Ash

Now before I go into a whole paragraph that sounds lowkey like a manifesto (for legal reasons, this is a joke). I would like to at least introduce myself to those who have no idea who the hell I am, who might see this. Hey, you never know.

I'm from NYC, and I have Puerto Rican, Filipino, and Italian parents. Like many gen z born in 02, my childhood was as much of a weird mix as my upbringing. My oldest memories were of cassettes and CDs, while spending time outside playing with nerf guns or some shit. I remember exactly where I was when Michael Jackson died, and then boom I got an iPod in the 6th grade. And from that moment forward, like many of you, the internet would change my life forever...

But by the time I was old enough to actually enjoy it fully, the internet had already begun to kick the bucket. Big corporations started to kill creativity among the individual. What a fucking bummer. It's like how as an '02 kid, I didn't get to have a high school graduation because of the COVID-19 pandemic (I will never shut up about that, btw).

I missed the short lived legacy of Myspace. However my tumblr blog reigned strong with it's basic level HTML, CSS and Java, and uplifted by it's loyal 1,000 followers. However, my rule would come to a deletion when my mental health went to shit in high school... why did I do that. With the knowledge I then learned later in college, voilà.

Now enough of the dramatic introduction! I won't say much else, I do not want to spoil the fun (also because I am partially improv-ing this, trust the process). However, I will say that I have many ideas. I have plans, projects, hopes and dreams that have been neglected, quite frankly. It's healing, to put it simply, to be able to finally push to see these come to fruition, big or small goals. I am stoked (and stoned) to share my journey with you, in this little part of the internet I can call my own.

Thanks for tuning in <3
